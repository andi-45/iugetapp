# **App Name**: OnBuch: Your Smart Study Buddy

## Core Features:

- Curriculum-Aligned Content: Provides courses and study sheets adapted to the official curriculum of Cameroon (MINESEC) for general secondary education (Years 2, 1, and final year - A, C, D series).
- Practice and Assessment: Offers quizzes, exercises, exam papers, and answer keys for self-assessment and practice.
- AI Virtual Professor: Includes an AI-powered virtual professor to explain lessons and correct homework using generative AI as a teaching tool.
- Personalized Revision Planner: Generates a personalized revision schedule to help students stay organized.
- Motivation and Stress Management: Provides motivational tips and tools to manage stress and enhance study habits.
- Offline Access: Offers an offline mode, allowing students to revise materials without an internet connection.
- Study Reminders: Delivers notifications to remind user of the personalized revision plans
- News Updates: Provides access to the latest educational news and updates.
- Resources and Libraries: Offers a curated library of educational resources and materials.
- Student Discovery: Enables students to discover and connect with peers for collaborative learning.

## Style Guidelines:

- A vibrant, energetic orange (#FF9500) to reflect enthusiasm and intellectual curiosity.
- Light-beige (#FAF9F6), to create a comfortable reading experience on the screen.
- A light-yellow (#FFE599), to bring additional pop to CTAs, buttons and progress bars.
- 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, clear presentation, complemented with 'Inter' for body text.
- Use clear and educational icons, in a consistent style, relevant to each subject area. Favor outline icons for clarity and a modern feel.
- The layout should be clean and intuitive, prioritizing easy navigation between courses, quizzes, and study schedules. Use a card-based layout for content organization.
- Employ subtle transitions and loading animations to enhance user experience without distracting from content. Use animations to celebrate completion of goals.
